From Bean to Brew

Founded in 2010 in Portland, what began as a small roastery has grown into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts. Our journey started with a simple mission: to celebrate the art of coffee by honoring traditional methods while embracing innovation.

Every batch we roast reflects our commitment to ethical sourcing, sustainable practices, and the relentless pursuit of flavor perfection.

"World's Best Boss" of Coffee

Though he once tried to rebrand espresso as "Michael's Little Hurricanes," his passion for connecting people through coffee is 100% genuine

Kevin Malone

Customer Experience & Cookie Ambassador

Our resident coffee-and-cookie pairing expert who believes "why use many word when coffee good?" Kevin keeps service simple: "You happy? Me happy." His famous chocolate chunk cookies have saved more rushed mornings than our emergency espresso shots.

Head Roaster & Latte Artist
